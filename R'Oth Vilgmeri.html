R'Oth Vilgmeri

R'oth Vilgmeri, known for its enormous trade, a keen eye for progression and technology, and of course, xenophobic inhabitants. The Vilgmeri are very good at solving problems, working in expansive trade, and drinking.
The immense power of the Vilgmeri lie in their progression and constant innovation, looking for ways to improve on what they already have. The best example of this being the great Titans, machines of war, capable of annihilating entire armies with a swing of their hand.
