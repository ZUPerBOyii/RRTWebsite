Gilded Juval

Golden Ramsen thrives on its fast-paced trade and the opulent lifestyles of its inhabitants. Despite being a hub of wealth and success, Golden Ramsen also harbors a darker side: it is notorious for producing some of the most skilled assassins in all of Amalon.

Celebrations are frequent in Golden Ramsen, with grand festivities occurring once every full moon. During these events, fireworks are launched into the sky, and the people of the city don vibrant and colorful clothing to dance and celebrate in hopes of becoming even wealthier.
