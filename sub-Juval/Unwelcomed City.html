Unwelcomed City

The Unwelcomed City is a ghostly and desolate place, once a vibrant and welcoming city that has now fallen into ruin and eternal darkness. It stands as a shadow of its former self, with remnants of its once bustling streets and lively community now laying dormant forevermore. The city has earned its eerie reputation due to its current state—abandoned, silent, and steeped in mystery. The reasons behind its downfall are largely unknown, but it has since become a place avoided by travelers and locals alike, with stories of a dark past surrounding it​.
