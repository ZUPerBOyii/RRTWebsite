Nur Oldamor

Nur Oldamor, also known as the "West Highlands," is a remote and rugged region within the Kingdom of R'Oth Vilgmeri. This area is characterized by its harsh terrain, making it difficult to access and navigate. The region's isolation has led to a lifestyle primarily focused on farming and mountain climbing, activities that sustain its small population.

Historically, Nur Oldamor held significant strategic importance, especially during the wars that plagued R'Oth Vilgmeri. Its elevated position made it an ideal lookout point, allowing the Vilgmeri or their invaders to monitor movements across the land.

Unfortunately, Nur Oldamor was not spared from the ravages of war. At one point, it was razed to the ground, a devastating blow to the local population and the kingdom's military capabilities. Following its destruction, the residents rebuilt their homes and cut off trade with the central regions of R'Oth Vilgmeri, further deepening the area's isolation.

Over the years, the once-barren landscape of Nur Oldamor began to recover. The land gradually returned to life, becoming fertile once again and providing a strong foundation for farming. This resurgence has allowed the region to regain some of its former prosperity, though it remains a quiet and solitary place, far removed from the bustling centers of power and commerce in R'Oth Vilgmeri.
