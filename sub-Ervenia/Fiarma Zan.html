Fiarma Zan

Fiarma Zan, known as the "Living City," is an unusual place marked by the coexistence of living and dead nature. One part of the city thrives with greenery and life, while another part lies dead and burning. This stark contrast gives the city its unique identity and atmosphere, embodying constant change.

Fiarma Zan is renowned for its connection to Ervenian culinary traditions. The Ervenian kitchen is the city's specialty, and its dishes are particularly popular among sailors who frequently pass through the harbors of Ervenia. A notable dish is roasted rima, a green bird native to the inner cities of Ervenia.
