Ertho Kal

Ertho Kal is a bustling city within Ervenia, located by the sea and renowned for its dense urban layout. Many of its inhabitants reside on the outskirts, while the city center is the heart of commerce and trade. What makes Ertho Kal stand out is not just its vibrant marketplace, but also the unique way in which its economy operates. Instead of relying on metallic currency, the locals use a form of wooden currency produced from special trees in the northern forests. The people of Ertho Kal are deeply connected with nature, carefully interacting with it without causing harm.

Among the notable landmarks of the city is the Initho Eza, also known as the "Trade Bringer," a large tower located in the center of Ertho Kal. This tower serves as a lighthouse, helping sailors navigate through the misty seas at night. Ertho Kal’s bay is constantly busy, with ships lining the shore to participate in the city’s bustling trade.

Visitors are often greeted warmly, a reflection of the city's kind and hospitable people. New arrivals are sometimes offered Yala, a sweet, light alcoholic drink that symbolizes a peaceful stay. The city's roots run deep into Ervenia's history, with a culture that values harmony with the natural world, making it a distinct and memorable location within the kingdom.
