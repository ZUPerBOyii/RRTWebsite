Gilded Juval

Gilded Juval is mostly known for its immense trade of products and gold. Home to the gigantic mines, they are one of the wealthiest kingdoms in Amalon.
The stoic Silver Knights are the backbone of the kingdom, resembling their might in not numbers, but in strategy and strength. Their major events mark the turning points in history, the most prevelant one being the story of Aintuli.
