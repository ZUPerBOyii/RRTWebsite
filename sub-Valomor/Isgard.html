Isgard

Isgard is an isolated city within the realm of Valomor, known primarily for its dedication to astral studies. This remote location is largely inaccessible and shrouded in mystery, with few details known beyond its purpose as a haven for devout scholars. These scholars dedicate themselves to uncovering the "secrets of the astral," spending many years in pursuit of advanced knowledge and mastery of this arcane field.

Despite its enigmatic reputation, Isgard is considered a place of intense academic and magical significance, offering a sanctuary for scholars who wish to immerse themselves in deep, uninterrupted study. Its isolation ensures that only the most determined and capable individuals make the journey to Isgard, further adding to the prestige associated with those who complete their studies there
