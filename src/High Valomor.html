High Valomor

High Valomor is known for its beautiful scenery, spanning across large territories. The major school of Applied Astral Arts is the most well known one
The primary two studies in Valomor are the Duelists, masters of astral weaponry, and the Sorcerers, the masters of astral magic. Aside from them, scholars and the magistrates lead the studies in their desired direction, constantly evolving.
