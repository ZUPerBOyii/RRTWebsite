High Valomor

High Valomor is known for its beautiful scenery, spanning across large territories. High Valomor is a kingdom known for its focus on knowledge, peace, and scholarship. It is depicted as a realm where education and intellectual pursuits are highly valued, and it remains distinct from the other kingdoms of Amalon due to its dedication to preserving wisdom and cultivating a peaceful society. The scholars of High Valomor are among the most revered, often training in advanced astral magic, which plays a crucial role in the kingdom's identity.
